# PRD: NutriStats Performance Redesign ## Product Vision A "Goal-Based Nutrition Execution" app for serious athletes (bodybuilders, physique/strength athletes). The app moves beyond simple logging to structured plan adherence and high-performance tracking. ## Target User Persona - **Primary:** Competitive bodybuilders and athletes who need precise macro control. - **Secondary:** Highly motivated individuals focused on disciplined fat loss or muscle gain. ## Key Design Principles 1. **Efficiency First:** Minimal friction for daily logging and repeating meals. 2. **Data Density:** Presenting a wealth of information (macros, calories, timing) without clutter. 3. **Disciplined Aesthetic:** Dark mode, high contrast, and a professional "tool" feel. 4. **Mobile-First:** Optimized for one-handed use and quick interactions during a busy training day. ## Functional Scope (MVP) ### 1. Nutrition Diary (Core) - Multiple meals per day (expandable/collapsible). - Real-time macro/calorie tracking against targets. - Quick-add from search, recent, or templates. ### 2. AI Auto Fill (New Feature) - Intelligent meal planning based on calorie/macro targets, meal count, and goal. - User provides preferences/exclusions. - Ability to "Swap Meal" while maintaining plan integrity. ### 3. Workout Tracking (New Section) - Cohesive planning and logging area for strength training. - Integrated performance data. ### 4. Saved Meals & Templates - Rapidly re-use high-performing meal combinations. - Template-driven workflow for consistent dieting. ### 5. Premium/Pro Area - High-level analytics. - Future-proofing for "Performance Analysis" (e.g., steroid analyzer placeholder). ## Screen Map 1. **Onboarding/Login:** Setting the tone and establishing athlete goals. 2. **Diary (Today):** The command center for the day's execution. 3. **Add Food / Search:** Fast, intuitive database access. 4. **Saved Meals:** Library of custom creations. 5. **Auto Fill Setup:** Defining parameters for the AI planner. 6. **Auto Fill Result:** Reviewing and tweaking the generated day. 7. **Workout Dashboard:** Performance overview and session entry. 8. **Profile/Premium:** Athlete settings and pro feature access.
